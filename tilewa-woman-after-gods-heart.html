a poem for her

Before the world knew her name,
God already loved her.

He knit her together in secret places —
her laugh, her eyes, her fire,
her stubborn tender heart.
Every thread deliberate. Every detail divine.

And today, on this day He set apart for her,
the whole of heaven looks down
and smiles at what He made.

She is not an accident of this earth.
She is an answer to a prayer
someone, somewhere, prayed without words —
and God, in His goodness, said: Tilewa.

a birthday blessing

May this year be the one where you finally see yourself
the way God has always seen you —

Chosen. Before you chose anything.
Loved. Before you earned anything.
Enough. Simply by being here.

May every door He opens find you ready.
May every hard season become your testimony.
May His joy be your strength on the ordinary days.

And may you never forget —
you are the woman God called by name,
the woman He fashioned for such a time as this.

Happy Birthday, Tilewa.
